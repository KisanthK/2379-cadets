Support Committee

Our Cadet Corps operates efficiently thanks to a dedicated team of officers, training staff, and support committee members.

Our support committee is a hardworking group of cadet parents who dedicate themselves to improving our corps in a variety of ways.

Their primary role is to raise and manage funds, while also helping plan memorable events such as the Christmas party, year-end picnic, comedy night, and selected March Break activities.

The committee also supports communications within and outside the corps, including the website, YouTube presence, and Facebook updates.

We are always looking for more interested and dedicated parents to join the team.

Fundraising & Support

Helping provide the resources, events, and added opportunities that strengthen the cadet experience.

Events & Community

Supporting seasonal and year-end activities that keep families and cadets engaged throughout the training year.

Communications

Assisting with public-facing communication across the website and social platforms.

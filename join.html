Before You Start

A Simple Process

We welcome youth ages 12–18 to come check us out on a Thursday evening between September and May. Training begins at 6:30 PM, and new cadets should arrive by 6:20 PM.

Follow the steps below before your first visit so your registration is sent to the correct unit and your arrival goes smoothly.

Joining Instructions

Step-by-Step Guide

Complete the registration first, then attend on a Thursday evening using the information below.

01

Go to the National Cadet Registration Website

Before attending, register through the National Cadet website and review the general information about the cadet program.

02

Enter the Correct Unit Name

In the Unit Name field, enter the full unit name exactly as shown below so your request is directed to our Administration Officer.
